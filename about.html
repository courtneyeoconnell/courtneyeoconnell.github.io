I specialize in thoughtful, strategic, clean, and empathetic design.

My name is Courtney O’Connell, and I’m a designer in Greenpoint, Brooklyn, NY. I’ve been designing for 6 years and love working collaboratively to design solutions that are measureable, code-conscious, and user-empathic. Although I’m currently a full-time Art Director at TheLadders, I am always interested in freelance work.

Designing takes up nearly all of my waking hours, but when I’m not designing I enjoy exploring the city, planning my next escape from the city, binge-watching docs on Netflix, sleeping, attending classes at Brooklyn Brainery, and planning my wedding.
